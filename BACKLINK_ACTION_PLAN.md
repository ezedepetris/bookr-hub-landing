## PRIORITY 5: HARO & Guest Posting

### 16. HARO (Help A Reporter Out)
**URL:** https://www.helpareporter.com  
**Time:** 30 min signup, 2-3 hours per week  
**Effort:** Medium  
**VALUE:** High-authority backlinks from news sites

**Steps:**
1. Sign up as a Source at helpareporter.com
2. Set up email alerts for categories:
   - Small Business
   - Software & Technology
   - Productivity
   - Beauty & Fashion
   - Entrepreneurs
3. Respond to 2-3 queries per week with helpful answers
4. Include a brief bio with link to BookrHub

**Example pitch:**

> 
> Quick answer to your question: [Answer]
> 
> Happy to provide more details or quotes for your article.
> 
> Best,
> [Your Name]
> BookrHub - https://bookrhub.com"
