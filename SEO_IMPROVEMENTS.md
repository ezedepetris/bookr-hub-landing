## Why You're Not Ranking Yet

### Primary Reasons:

1. **New Domain Age** ⏰
   - Google typically takes 3-6 months to fully index and rank new sites
   - Domain authority builds over time

2. **Zero Backlinks** 🔗
   - Backlinks are a major ranking factor
   - You need quality backlinks from relevant sites

3. **Competitive Keyword** 🏆
   - "Booking system" has high competition
   - Established players (Calendly, Acuity, etc.) have years of SEO work

4. **Limited Content** 📄
   - More comprehensive content helps rankings
   - Your page is well-structured but could be more detailed

5. **Not Indexed Yet** 🔍
   - Google may not have fully crawled/indexed your site
   - Check: `site:bookrhub.com` in Google search
