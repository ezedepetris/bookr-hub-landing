Frequently Asked Questions

What percentage of NZ salons use online booking?
Approximately 60% of NZ personal care businesses currently offer online booking. Adoption is highest among beauty salons and spas (72%) and lower among barbershops (52%) and nail salons (48%). Auckland leads all regions at 72% adoption.
How big is the NZ beauty and salon market?
The NZ hair and beauty services market is worth approximately $1.2 billion annually, with about 8,500 businesses operating in the sector. The industry employs around 25,000 people and grows at approximately 4.7% per year.
What payment methods do NZ customers prefer?
Credit and debit cards remain the most popular at 55% of transactions. However, New Zealand has one of the highest BNPL adoption rates globally, with 32% of consumers having used Laybuy for services. Digital wallets (12%) and cash (8%) make up the remainder.
Is Laybuy available for salon bookings?
Yes, Laybuy is widely available for salon bookings in New Zealand. Many booking platforms, including BookrHub, integrate with Laybuy to allow customers to spread payments over time. This is particularly popular for higher-value services like hair colouring, lash extensions, and spa treatments.
