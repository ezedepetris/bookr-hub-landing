The No-Show Problem

No-shows represent significant revenue loss for personal trainers. A missed session means lost income that can't be recovered.

14% Average no-show rate for training sessions Source: Industry Survey 2025

At $75 average per session, a trainer with 30 weekly clients losing 14% to no-showes loses approximately $1,125 monthly ($13,500 annually).
