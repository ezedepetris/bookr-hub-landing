The No-Show Problem

No-shows represent significant revenue loss for massage therapists. A single missed 90-minute session can cost $150 or more in lost revenue.

At $100 average per session, a massage therapist with 30 weekly appointments losing 14% to no-showes loses approximately $1,400 monthly ($16,800 annually).
