The No-Show Problem

No-shows represent one of the most significant revenue leaks in the salon industry. Understanding the scope of this problem is the first step to solving it.

14% Average no-show rate for salon appointments Source: Industry Survey 2025

At $65 average per visit (color, cuts, styling), a salon with 100 weekly appointments losing 14% to no-showes loses approximately $910 weekly ($4,680 monthly, $56,160 annually).
