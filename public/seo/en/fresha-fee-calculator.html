Frequently Asked Questions

Does BookrHub really charge 0% commission?
Yes. BookrHub charges a flat monthly subscription with absolutely no commission on bookings. Whether you take 10 appointments or 1000 in a month, you pay the same price. Your revenue is yours to keep.
What's the catch with Fresha's "free" platform?
The catch is their commission model. While the platform itself is free to join, Fresha takes 5-8% of every booking you process through them. For a salon doing $10,000/month in bookings, that's $600-$800 per month—or $7,200-$9,600 per year going to Fresha.
Are there any other fees with Fresha?
Yes. Beyond their booking commission, Fresha also charges payment processing fees (typically 2-3% additional) for credit card transactions. These fees can bring the total cost to 7-11% of your booking revenue. BookrHub includes payment processing in many plans with very low fees.
