Booking Flow Examples

See how different businesses customize their booking flow:

Barbershop Flow

  1. Select service (Cut, Cut + Beard, Beard Only)
  2. Choose barber (or "First Available")
  3. Pick date and time
  4. Enter name and phone
  5. Pay $10 deposit (optional)
  6. Receive confirmation

Hair Salon Flow

  1. Browse service categories (Cut, Color, Treatments)
  2. Select specific service (Balayage, $220)
  3. Choose stylist (or "Any Available")
  4. View available dates (next 2 weeks)
  5. Pick time slot
  6. Create account or continue as guest
  7. Add notes for stylist
  8. Confirm booking

Spa Flow

  1. Select treatment type (Massage, Facial, Body)
  2. Choose specific treatment (60-min Deep Tissue, $130)
  3. Select therapist preference
  4. Pick date from calendar
  5. Choose time (morning, afternoon, evening)
  6. Enter client details
  7. Add special requests
  8. Review and confirm
  9. Pay 30% deposit

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize my booking page to match my brand?
Absolutely. BookrHub offers full customization of your booking page, including your logo, brand colors, fonts, and imagery. You can also choose from dozens of professional templates designed for different business types, or work with our team to create a completely custom landing page.
Do you offer landing page templates?
Yes! We have dozens of professionally designed templates ready to use. Simply choose a template, customize colors and branding, add your services—and you're live. All templates are mobile-optimized and SEO-friendly. If you need something unique, our team can help create a fully custom landing page.
Can I customize how my booking flow works?
Yes. BookrHub lets you customize every step of your booking flow. Choose what services appear, how clients select staff, what information you collect, what payment options are available, and more. Whether you need a simple 3-step flow or a complex multi-service booking process, we've got you covered.
Do I need my own website to use BookrHub?
No. Your BookrHub booking page serves as your online presence. It includes your services, staff, pricing, photos, and booking functionality—all in one place. You can add this link to your Instagram bio, Facebook page, or any existing website. Many of our customers use their BookrHub page as their primary website.
Can I use my own domain name for booking?
Yes. While you get a free URL (yourshop.bookrhub.com), you can upgrade to use your own custom domain (book.yourshop.com or bookings.yourshop.com). This looks more professional and makes it easier for clients to find and remember your booking page.
How does the deposit system work?
You can require clients to pay a deposit when booking—either a percentage of the service price or a fixed amount. Deposits are credited toward the service and forfeited if the client no-shows. This reduces no-shows by 60-80% and protects your time. You set the deposit rules, and BookrHub handles everything automatically.
What payment methods can I accept?
BookrHub supports multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, local payment methods, MercadoPago (for LATAM), Stripe, and more. You can offer full payment, deposits only, pay-at-venue options, or split payments. All payment processing is handled securely through our integrated payment partners.
Do you charge commission on bookings?
No. BookrHub charges a flat monthly subscription with zero commission on bookings. Unlike platforms like Fresha that take 5-8% of every booking, you keep 100% of what you earn. The only fees are standard payment processing costs.
Can I manage multiple staff members?
Yes. BookrHub includes comprehensive staff management: individual calendars, service qualifications, working hours, time-off requests, role-based permissions, and performance tracking. Each staff member can have their own login and booking page, or you can manage everything under one account.
What if I need help setting up?
We offer setup assistance to help you get started. Our team can help configure your services, customize your booking flow, design your landing page, and more. Check your plan for included setup support, or contact us about premium onboarding services.
