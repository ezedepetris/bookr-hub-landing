# Linkable Asset Ideas for BookrHub

**Goal:** Create 10 comprehensive, data-driven resources that other websites will naturally cite and link to.

## Why These Will Get Links

1. **Original data** - From BookrHub's 700+ businesses (you have this data)
2. **Actionable insights** - Not available elsewhere
3. **Comprehensive coverage** - Become the definitive resource
4. **Non-commercial intent** - Purely helpful, no sales pitch
