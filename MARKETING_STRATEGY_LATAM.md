## 🚀 Posting Schedule

### Week 1-2: JOIN GROUPS
- Join 5-10 groups per day
- Don't post yet - just observe

### Week 3: SOFT POSTS
- 1 post per day
- Focus on VALUE, not sales
- Answer questions

### Week 4+: ACTIVE POSTING
- 2-3 posts per day
- Rotate groups
- Answer EVERY comment within 1 hour

### Best Times to Post (Argentina)
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: 7-9 PM (after work)
- **Saturday**: 10 AM - 12 PM
- **Avoid**: Monday mornings, Sunday
